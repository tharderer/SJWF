    A Christian hurt me.

    You: I'm so sorry that happened. Would you accept my apology, for those who did that to you?

    You: (If appropriate:) Have you ever tried to love somebody and made a mess out of it? You had good intentions, but everything went wrong. Do you think there was a possibility in your friend's desire for you to know Jesus that she just went about it the wrong way?

    You: Jesus would not approve of rude behavior either. By the way, what is your understanding of who Jesus is?
